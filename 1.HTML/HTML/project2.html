Project 2 - HTML Reflections

I moved from Puerto Rico to the continental United States when I was about 9 or 10. That was around 2002 or 2003. At that time all of the "American" kids were reading Harry Potter and I so badly wanted to fit in. To me Harry Potter is reminiscent of a new life.

I didn't read Dune until I was much older. I read it when the movie came out. I didn't think I would like the movie but when I saw it I thoroughly enjoyed it and thought I would give the book a chance. It's definitely not for everybody; it is very slow to start.

Having been deployed to Afghanistan around 2014, I read A Thousand Splendid Suns and it was so good. It helped give me better insight on how the women of Afghanistan live.
